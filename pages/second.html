What is PyMRCE?

PyMRCE is an affiliate of HydPy. HydPy is a regional chapter and a non-profit open source community where people from different professional backgrounds come together and collaborate to work on a project by working solving the real world problems. HydPy is a chapter or a regional society of PSSI (Python Software Society Of India), whose main aim is to increase the number of python user in India and also conduct different Conferences, Workshops, Meetups to increase the awareness about this Beginner-friendly language. Under the Python Express initiative, by the Hydpy, PyMRCE is an Open Source Community for all the Python Lovers and Under Graduation Students. PyMRCE also helps the UnderGrads find their fields of interests in the fields of IT. By conducting Meetups, Workshops and Conferences frequently, getting all the Pythonistas and enthusiastic UnderGrads under a roof and collaborate on a project PyMRCE serves as one of the Elite Non-Profit Community for different Programmers, Developers and IT Enthusiasts.
